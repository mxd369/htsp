What is the next step in the following evaluation?

Step 0: (/ (* 3 4) (+ (- 7 4) 3))

Step 1: (/ 12 (+ (- 7 4) 3))

Step 2: (/ 12 (+ 3 3))

Step 3: (/ 12 6)

Step 4: _________

2

ANSWER: 2

EXPLANATION:

All of the operands to / have been evaluated, so the primitive can be called with the arguments 12 and 6.
